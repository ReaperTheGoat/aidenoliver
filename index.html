How YOU Can Save Water ğŸ’¡

Fix Leaks

A dripping tap can waste over 10,000 liters of water a year. Fixing leaks is the simplest step we can take at home.

Use Buckets

Wash your car or water your plants with a bucket instead of a hose. A hosepipe can waste hundreds of liters in minutes.

Reuse Water

Reuse greywater (like from washing dishes or laundry) for gardens or flushing toilets where possible.

Take Short Showers

Cutting your shower time by just 2 minutes can save up to 20 liters of water each day.
