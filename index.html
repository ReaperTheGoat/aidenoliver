Time Management for Teens

Simple tips, a daily planner, and quick Pomodoro guides to help you study smarter — not harder.

Top Quick Tips

  • Use Pomodoro: 25 minutes focused, 5 minutes break. Repeat 4 times, then take a longer break.
  • Plan the night before: List 3 important tasks for tomorrow.
  • Two-minute rule: If it takes less than 2 minutes, do it now.
  • Batch similar tasks: Do homework of same subject together to save time.
  • Turn off distractions: Put phone on Do Not Disturb while studying.

Daily Example Schedule

A simple school day plan — adjust to your classes.

  • 06:30 Wake up + morning routine (20–30min)
  • 07:30 School
  • 16:00 Snack + 30min break
  • 16:30–18:00 Homework (use 2 Pomodoro cycles)
  • 18:00–19:00 Hobby / exercise
  • 20:00 Review notes (20 min) + prep for tomorrow
  • 21:30 Wind down + sleep
